You need to pass a secure English language test (SELT)

You’ll need to prove that you can read, write, speak and understand English to at least level B1 on the CEFR scale ('independent user'). 

Finding and booking a test

It can take a few weeks to book a test and you’ll need to pay a fee.

  1. Find what tests you can take
  2. Find an approved test centre to take your test

If you’ve passed a test in the past 2 years, you can use that instead of taking a new one.

Once you’ve booked and taken your test, you’ll get a SELT unique reference number to include in your application.
